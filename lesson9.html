Project Name: Engineering Final Project
Date: October 26, 2025

Summary

For my senior design, I built a laptop-free programmable system that precisely controls speed and movement of a motor controller.

Iteration

The initial prototype was an Arduino controlled motor controller with 2 buttons and a potentiometer speed controller all on a breadboard circuit. But the jumper wires are really loose and short, the potentiometer was very sensitive and didn't allow mid spin speed change, and the overall setup was hard to access/use.

Figure 3: CAD model of the revised, reinforced motor mount.

Conclusion

The final design successfully met all project requirements. The motor mount is stable, lightweight, and easy to assemble. The use of external CSS and a clear page layout made the final report easy to read and understand, fulfilling the project's documentation goals.
